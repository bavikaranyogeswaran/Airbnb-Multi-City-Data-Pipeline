The Madrid Airbnb market is sizable, with 25,000 total listings and 10,453 unique hosts. The median nightly rate is €110.0, with prices ranging from €70.0 at the 25th percentile to €164.0 at the 75th percentile, and reaching as high as €343.0 at the 95th percentile. This spread indicates a diverse range of options for guests, from budget-friendly to luxury accommodations.

In terms of supply structure, entire homes make up 66.8% of the listings, while private rooms account for 32.3%. A significant proportion of hosts, 21.6%, are superhosts, indicating a high level of quality and service. The median availability is 172.0 days per year, and 32.3% of listings are highly available, with 270 or more days per year. This suggests that many hosts are committed to providing consistent and reliable accommodations for guests.

The guest experience in Madrid is generally positive, with a median rating of 4.75 out of 5 and a median of 11.0 reviews per listing. The top neighbourhoods for listings are Embajadores, Universidad, and Palacio, indicating a high demand for accommodations in these areas. As the Madrid Airbnb market continues to evolve, the data suggests that hosts who focus on providing high-quality, consistently available accommodations in desirable neighbourhoods will be well-positioned for success.
