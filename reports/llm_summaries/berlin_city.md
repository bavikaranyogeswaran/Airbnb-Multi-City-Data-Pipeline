Berlin's short-term rental market is sizable, with 14,274 total listings and 9,464 unique hosts. The median nightly rate is €104.0, indicating a relatively affordable option for guests. However, prices can vary significantly, with the 25th percentile at €70.0 and the 75th percentile at €160.0, while the 95th percentile reaches €300.0, showing a wide range of options for different budgets.

The supply structure of Berlin's market is dominated by entire homes, which make up 67.7% of listings, while private rooms account for 30.8%. A notable 24.7% of hosts are superhosts, suggesting a high level of quality and service. The median availability is 112.0 days per year, and 27.3% of listings are highly available, with 270 or more days per year. This indicates a mix of dedicated hosts and those who may be renting out their properties on a more occasional basis.

Guests in Berlin are generally very satisfied, with a median rating of 4.86 out of 5. Listings have a median of 8.0 reviews, providing a good amount of feedback for potential guests. The most popular neighbourhoods for listings are Alexanderplatz, Frankfurter Allee Süd FK, and Tempelhofer Vorstadt, suggesting that hosts are catering to guests who want to be in the heart of the action. As the Berlin market continues to evolve, the data suggests that hosts who can offer high-quality, well-located entire homes will be well-positioned to attract guests and drive revenue.
